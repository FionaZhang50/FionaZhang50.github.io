    • About Me

    I am a student at the University of Southern California studying Computer Science who is interested in the intersectionality of art and computer science. Ever since I was young I have always been a visual artist, drawing characters and scenery. Then in high school, I discovered computer science, a new skill and form of language that also allowed me to express myself but in a different way. Computer science provided me with the skills to create new products and my own projects, such as a game program, a calculator, machine learning applications, and a website. Besides art and computer science, I also enjoy reading, playing badminton, and watching movies.

    CS Languages: C++/C, Java, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, Python

    Art Mediums: Digital Illustration, Watercolor, Oil, Acrylic, Sketch Pencil, Pastel, Chinese Painting
